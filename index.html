Short-bio

  Yihang She is a fourth year undergraduate in Nanjing University in China, majoring in Geographic Information Science. He spent his spring semester of 2018 in UC Berkeley as an exchange student and in the summer he visited Environment and Society Research Group in the University of Winnipeg in Canada as a Mitacs Globalink Research Intern. He was also a Resident Undergraduate Student of Institute for Advanced Studies in Humanities and Social Sciences (IAS) in Nanjing University and a research intern of NASA's Ames Research Center. Yihang is currently working on his graduation thesis in Urban and Thermal Remote Sensing Group for his Bachelor's Degree. He is expected to graduate from Nanjing University in July 2019.
